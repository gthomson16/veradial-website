## Notes on choices

- **Bullet personas** — easier to scan on mobile than paragraph form
- **Named competitors kept** — captures "Google Voice alternative" / "OpenPhone alternative" search intent (real, indexed by Google Play)
- **"Never used to train AI models" kept** — verifiable from your privacy policy and a real differentiator in 2026
- **Removed "under 5 minutes" claim** — not always operationally true (number search/setup time varies)
- **No emojis** — matches your operator-focused, no-fluff brand voice
- **No fake testimonials** — pending real customer reviews
