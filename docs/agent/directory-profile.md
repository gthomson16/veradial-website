## Long Description

VeraDial is an AI-powered business phone app built for freelancers, solopreneurs, contractors, real estate agents, recruiters, sales teams, and small business owners who run their business from their phone.

Users get a dedicated US or Canadian business number with AI outbound calling, business SMS, voicemail transcription, call recording, call forwarding, call screening, and caller ID control. Instead of just giving you a second number, VeraDial lets an AI assistant make routine business calls on your behalf and returns a transcript and summary after each call.

It is designed for people who need to confirm appointments, follow up with leads, schedule callbacks, and keep personal and business communication separate without carrying a second phone.
